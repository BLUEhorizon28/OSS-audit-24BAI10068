THE OPEN SOURCE MANIFESTO
Date: 30 March 2026
--------------------------------------
I believe that software should be a shared resource for humanity.
Every day, I rely on open-source foundations like github. It serves as a reminder
that the best and most secure technology is built transparently.
To me, the essence of open source is non vititan. It represents the right to
inspect, learn, and adapt the digital tools that govern our lives.
If given the opportunity, I would build my projects and release it under a
permissive license so others can stand on my shoulders, just as I have
stood on the shoulders of the developers who came before me.
